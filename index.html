LGBTQ+ INITIATIVES

The Director of LGBTQ+ Initiatives will work closely with us as well as the university in order to ensure the fair and equal treatment of LGBTQ+ and their allies.

You will continue to pursue the initiatives you have already worked on as well as brainstorm with us and our Director of Education on new ways to implement education and acceptance. If elected, Directors will have weekly meetings with me in order to stay updated on the work you will be doing!
