PLATFORM

Click on a platform to learn more!

DIVERSITY

By creating a diversity-based activity, we will promote education and understanding between cultures at CMU.

Through this, we hope to increase cultural equality on campus.

STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS

We hope to strengthen and create more successful student organizations to promote student involvement on campus.

We will become directly involved with young organizations and the training of new members.

LGBTQ+ INITIATIVES

We will be working with the University, as well as the newly approved LGBTQ+ residence hall, to ensure fair and equal treatment of all LGBTQ+ students in the campus community.

STUDENT DEBT AWARENESS

We hope to better prepare students to deal with their debt.

Workshops will be held to ensure students are educated and ready to deal with their savings, investments, and spending.

SARVP
AWARENESS

We hope to increase awareness and education about relationship and sexual abuse on campus.

We will do this by strengthening the available education materials about these topics.

USNWR RANKINGS

It is our goal to raise the US News and World Report ranking of Carnegie Mellon University.

We will do this by encouraging student giving and focusing on other low-ranking areas.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

We hope to strengthen the connection between international students and student government.

Our hope is to make CMU a more comfortable place for students of all nationalities and cultures.

EDUCATION INNOVATION

We hope to promote on-campus education about intrapersonal skills on campus.

One of our focuses will be on professional communications, such as emails and resumes.

GRADUATE STUDENT ADVANCEMENT

We believe graduate students are thoroughly under-represented in student government.

We hope to include the other large percentage of the student body in our decision making.
